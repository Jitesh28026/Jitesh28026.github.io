Process

How I tackle
complex problems.

🔍 01

I start with the constraint, not the canvas

Most problems aren't unsolved, they're unseen. I spend the first phase finding what's actually blocking users, not what they say is blocking them. Usually those are different things.

📍 02

I design for the person who hates reading instructions

Enterprise users are busy and context-switching constantly. If a flow needs explanation, it's not done yet.

⚡ 03

I treat "coming soon" as a design failure

Shipped is better than perfect, but shipping something nobody uses is worse than waiting. I push for small real releases so I'm always learning from actual behavior.

♻️ 04

I document the decisions, not just the outcome

The file isn't the work. The reasoning behind every tradeoff is.
