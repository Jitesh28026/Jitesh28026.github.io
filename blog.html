01

Structured Data Markup

02

Craft unique title tags (50–60 chars) and meta descriptions (150–160 chars) for every single page.

03

Internal Linking Architecture

Link related content together with descriptive anchor text to help crawlers understand your site's hierarchy.

04

Use next-gen formats (WebP, AVIF), proper sizing, and native lazy loading to slash load times dramatically.
