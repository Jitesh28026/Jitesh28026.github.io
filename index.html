About

Designer who thinks in systems.

I'm Jitesh, a product designer based in Bengaluru. I work at the intersection of UX, product thinking, and prototyping — with a focus on B2B applications where the users are experts and every interaction needs to earn its place.

My day-to-day involves untangling complex catalogue structures, building filter systems that don't overwhelm, and occasionally convincing engineers why that 4px difference matters.

How I work

Process over perfection.

01
Understand

Start with the user's mental model and the business constraint. No wireframe before the problem is sharp.

02
Map

Information architecture, user flows, edge cases. The boring stuff that makes the final design feel obvious.

03
Prototype

High-fidelity, interactive. Real content, real constraints. Test it before you build it.

04
Iterate

Ship phase one, learn from it, refine. Done is better than perfect — but done badly isn't shipped.
