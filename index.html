Process

How I tackle
complex problems.

🔍 01

Deep starts

I spend a lot of time upfront just trying to understand the problem. I dig into what users need and what the business wants so I can focus on building the most impactful solution from the start.

📍 02

Balanced decisions

I don't just follow one source of truth. I make decisions by mixing research data with business goals and my own gut feeling from building real products. It's about finding a solution that actually works in the real world.

⚡ 03

Ship to learn

I believe the best insights come from real-world usage. I aim to ship the initial product as soon as it's ready, because you only truly understand what a user wants once they start using your design in their day-to-day life.

♻️ 04

Real-world refinement

For me, the design isn't "done" at launch. I use the feedback and behavior from the live product to iterate and pivot. Shipping is just the beginning of the process, not the end.
